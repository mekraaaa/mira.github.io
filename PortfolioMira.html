SMART ROBOT

Features

  • Color Detection
  • Remotely Controlled
  • Line Follower
  • Temperature & Humidity Detection
  • Obstacle Detector

    • PLC TESTING STATION

    Overview

    The testing station is in charge of identifying the color, material, and height of workpieces before sending them on to the next workstation. The testing station is in charge of the following tasks. Obtaining information to determining whether the workpiece is red, metal, or black, and measuring height, and comparing specified attributes to reference values, and making a conclusion reject black and red workpieces and pass metal workpieces. Apart from these features, the testing station must be able to track the position of workpieces and transport them from the sensing module to the height measurement module via the lift module and ejecting pistons, and then to the next station via the slide module.
